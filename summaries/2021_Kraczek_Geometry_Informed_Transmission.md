## Mathematics

Example equation using LaTeX notation:

$$P_t = P_0 \left(\frac{d}{d_0}\right)^{\alpha}$$

Where $P_t$ is the transmit power, $d$ is the distance, $d_0$ is a reference distance, and $\alpha$ is the path loss exponent.
